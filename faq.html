Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is telemedicine?
Telemedicine is the use of technology to provide medical care remotely. Patients can consult doctors without visiting a hospital or clinic.
2. Is my information safe and private?
Yes, your medical data and conversations are encrypted and handled under strict confidentiality and data protection laws.
3. What kind of issues can be treated through telemedicine?
Common illnesses such as infections, skin conditions, mental health consultations, chronic condition management, and more.
4. Can I get prescriptions through this platform?
Yes. After your consultation, you can receive e-prescriptions that can be used at our partner pharmacies or delivered to you.
5. How do I book an appointment?
Click on the “Book Appointment” button on the homepage, fill in the required form, and select a convenient time slot.
6. Can I speak with the same doctor again?
Yes. You may request a follow-up with the same doctor depending on availability and your medical history.
7. What should I do in an emergency?
Telemedicine is not suitable for emergencies. Please go to the nearest hospital or call emergency services immediately.
8. How do payments work?
You will see payment options during the appointment booking process. We support various secure payment methods.
9. Can I use this service outside my country?
Yes, if local regulations permit. Please check with our support team if you are located outside our main service region.
10. What devices are supported?
Our platform works on mobile phones, tablets, and computers with internet access and a browser.
